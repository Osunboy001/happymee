Privacy Policy

Last updated: February 15, 2026

Introduction

Welcome to Happy Me We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our website.

Information We Collect

We do NOT collect personal information. Happy Me does not require account creation, and we do not collect names, email addresses, or any personally identifiable information.

We only store:

  • Country Preference: Your selected country is saved in your browser's local storage.
  • Alert Preference: Whether you've seen our one-time alert is saved in local storage.

Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Imgflip API: To display meme images. View Imgflip Privacy Policy

Cookies and Local Storage

We use browser local storage (not cookies) to remember your country preference and alert status. This information never leaves your device and is not sent to our servers.

Data Security

Since we don't collect personal information, there is minimal risk to your privacy. Your preferences are stored locally in your browser and can be cleared at any time.

Your Rights

You can clear your stored preferences at any time by clearing your browser's local storage or cache.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page.
